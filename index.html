Introduction

Hi! I'm Dena El-Giar, a Canadian-Egyptian artist based in New Orleans. I'm a crafts enthusiast and pride myself in putting my heart and soul into everything I create. I am currently a chemical engineering student at Tulane University.

Passion

I love painting and creating unique pieces for any occasion. Whether it's a gift or just something to hang in your room, I do it all. Feel free to contact me on social media, or email.
